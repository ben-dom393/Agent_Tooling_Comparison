TASK 3: IMPROVED MODEL
================================================================================

Prompt
------
Diagnose baseline weaknesses, propose up to 3 improvements, implement the most
promising change(s), and compare fairly against the baseline.

Diagnosis
---------
  1. LR assumes linearity -- cannot capture non-linear/interaction effects
  2. pdays=999 sentinel treated as real numeric distance in LR
  3. Default 0.5 decision threshold is suboptimal for imbalanced data

Proposed Improvements
---------------------
  1. HistGradientBoostingClassifier (sklearn native, LightGBM-equivalent)
  2. Better feature engineering (pdays_clean, ordinal education, high_campaign flag)
  3. CV-based threshold tuning (maximise F1 on inner folds, avoid test leakage)

Actions Taken

1. Checked for XGBoost/LightGBM -- not installed.
